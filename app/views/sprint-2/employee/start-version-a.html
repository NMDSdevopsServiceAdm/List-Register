Apply to join the Adult Social Care Workforce Register (ASC-Workforce Register)

As a member of the adult social care sector you deserve equal recognition to your NHS colleagues. Joining the ASC-Workforce Register is the first step in helping us to achieve this.

What is the ASC-Workforce Register?

The register is a list of staff working in adult social care in England. It is run by Skills for Care, in partnership with the Department of Health and Social Care.

What are the benefits to signing up?

You will become part of a valued professional community, enabling us to build a more confident workforce.

The register will allow us to update you on important information, such as the latest government guidance. It will also provide ways to support your wellbeing and in the future, give you access to training opportunities to help you further your career.

We may also use the register to let you know about discounts you are eligible for as part of the adult social care workforce.

Who should be on the register?

All adult social care staff.

How do I join the register?

You just need to tell us your name, job role and give us permission to send you information on the email address, or mobile number, you would prefer to be contacted on.

You will then be asked to provide details of your employer so we can verify you currently work in adult social care. If this is confirmed your application is complete and you will then be added to the register.

What will we use this data for?

We will only use these contact details to send you information. They will not be used for any other purpose and we will not share them with anyone else.
