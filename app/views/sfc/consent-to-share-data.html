Consent to share data with the Care Register

What is the Care Register?

The register is a list of staff working in adult social care in England. It is run by Skills for Care, in partnership with the Department of Health and Social Care.

How does it work?

The register will provide a way for us to get in touch with the adult social care workforce so we can send them important messages and support improvement in the sector.

Who should be on the register?

All adult social care staff should join the register.

Data required

We need your permission to share the name, or ID number, and job role from the information you have recorded about your staff in the Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set.
